Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

FORJA is a fitness tracking progressive web application operated by Daniel Ngr. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use FORJA ("the App").

Contact: hello@danielngr.dev

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use FORJA:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you sign in with Google or create an account.
  • Fitness data: Workout sessions, exercise logs, sets, reps, weight, personal records, and training load data that you enter into the App.
  • Profile data: Age, weight, height, fitness goal, experience level, and preferred language that you provide during setup.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to understand how features are used (no personally identifiable information).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your workout data across devices via Supabase cloud storage.
  • To personalize your training routine based on your profile and goals.
  • To display your progress, personal records, and training load metrics.
  • To improve the App features and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places:

  • Locally on your device via browser localStorage — accessible only on your device.
  • Supabase cloud database — encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Supabase complies with GDPR and SOC 2 Type II. Data is stored in servers in the European Union.

5. Third-Party Services

FORJA uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in. Privacy Policy
  • BunnyCDN (bunny.net) — video delivery for exercise demonstrations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time via the App settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by using the "Reset" option in the App or by contacting us.
  • Export your workout data using the "Export" button in the App.
  • Correct inaccurate data by updating your profile in the App.

For EU residents, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for up to 2 years.

8. Children's Privacy

FORJA is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, disclose, or sell. We do not sell personal information. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you may request to know, delete, or opt-out of the sale of your personal data by contacting us at hello@danielngr.dev.
